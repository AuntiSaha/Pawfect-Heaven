Frequently Asked Questions

Booking a service is easy! Simply browse our services, select the one you need, choose your preferred provider, and schedule a time that works for you. You can book online or through our mobile app.

Yes, all our providers undergo thorough background checks, reference verification, and in-person interviews before they're approved to join our platform. Your pet's safety is our top priority.

You can cancel or reschedule appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. For cancellations within 24 hours, a small fee may apply depending on the service provider's policy.

All payments are processed securely through our platform. You'll be charged after the service is completed. We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Tips for providers can be added through the app after service completion.

Many of our providers have experience with special needs pets. When booking, you can specify any special requirements your pet may have, and we'll match you with providers who have the appropriate experience and skills.
